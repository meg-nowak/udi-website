Udi: Quality Tutoring.


Providing quality online tutoring for New Zealand students.

Udi is a platform that connects students with tutors who are at a top level in their teaching subjects. We offer in-person tutoring in Auckland as well as online tutoring for students across the country. Have friends that you want to be tutored alongside with, or would you rather have multiple tutors to help you out? We also offer group tutoring for multiple students, or a student with multiple tutors to help students receive understanding from a broad range of perspectives.
