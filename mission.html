Our Mission


Making quality tutoring accessible.

Udi was founded in May 2019 to help New Zealand students excel academically through personalised tutoring. We understand that each student has their own way of learning and their own academic goals to strive for. Our role is to deliver the most bespoke education to them so that they can maximise their learning and conquer their goals.

  1. All of our tutors have achieved the highest grade in the subjects they teach.
  2. Our tutors cut to the chase, simplify the complex, and are enthused about working with students to achieve their academic goals.
  3. We have rigorously screened every tutor on their behaviour and conduct.
  4. Our pricing is competitive and discounts are available for bulk purchases.
  5. We offer nation-wide online tutoring and Auckland-based in-person tutoring.
  6. Our students receive ongoing support and mentorship from their tutors.

Total hours of tutoring completed by our tutors: 350 +
